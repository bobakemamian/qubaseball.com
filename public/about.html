About us

OVERVIEW

As alumni, we are very grateful for our experiences at Quinnipiac. It has shaped us as people. We’d like to pay it forward.

The Quinnipiac Baseball Booster Club is a CT registered nonstock company at the service of the Quinnipiac University Baseball Program.

Our Mission

Go to Omaha. Win in Omaha.

OUR STRUCTURE

Our purpose is to provide community encouragement and financial support for the Quinnipiac University Baseball Team.
